HOW TO PLAY

NostraJAMus is a platform for music discovery, prediction, and tastemaking!

The concept is simple:

Discover

Pick any song you want from an official Soundcloud account.

Sign Up

Enter your song into our 2015 Kickoff Contest for free. We’ll only use your personal info to send you awesome prizes.

Win

If your song wins the most Jam Points, we’ll reward you with cash, swag, and fame. Actually though real money.


Scoring

The Basics

Your song wins Jam Points based on playcount and artist popularity. The song with the most Jam Points at the end of the contest wins.

The best songs begin with fewer streams and are by lesser-known artists. Balancing artist popularity with play counts will be important. Like deciding between a donut and a bagel. We have our best people working on the ideal balance, but for now just pick your favorite jam.

The NostraJAMus Story

  • Fall 2014

    Tony and Collin go hiking

    iTunes is a teenager. But radio is still the most effective way to launch new artists and glance at our culture’s musical landscape. This wouldn’t bum us out so much by itself, but the 10 most popular songs on the radio receive double the airtime today than those of ten years ago. There are more ways to create and consume music than ever before. The funnel of popular music should not be narrowing. It should be expanding. That’s why we created NostraJAMus.

  • Fall 2014

    Born Winners: NostraJAMus wins 2nd place in UVA E-Cup

    NostraJAMus is an online platform for music discovery, predictions, and tastemaking. The concept is simple: find a new song you like by an unheralded artist? Support the track on our site. Enter that song in a contest, submit it in a duel with a friend, share it on your profile. We want you to champion the undiscovered musicians dotting the globe. And we want to reward you.

  • Early 2015

    Developing a sweet product

    NostraJAMus will give you prizes when your song selections jump up the charts. We’re here to incentivize music discovery and democratize tastemaking. We think that ‘big data’ and sterile analysis of listening habits have hampered new artists, and we’re here to return the power to listeners like you. Oh, and also, we’re a bunch of college kids who love music. For now, we’ll be running open contests on our site as we build out other features like duels, profiles, and tastemaker rankings.

  • Future

    So here’s our advice right now: spend an extra half hour this week listening to new songs. Songs you’ve never heard before. Songs by artists with weird names. Songs outside your go-to genres. And if you find one that electrifies, submit it in our newest contest.
