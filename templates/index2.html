HOW TO PLAY

We offer a portal for music discovery, prediction, and tastemaking!

The concept is simple:

Discover

Find your new jam with our soundcloud based search. The best songs are those from lesser known artists that are poised to explode.

Predict

Support that track on our site by entering it in a contest, submitting it in a duel, or sharing it on your profile.

Profit

As your song rises in popularity you earn jam points and get rewarded with cash, swag, and social cred.
